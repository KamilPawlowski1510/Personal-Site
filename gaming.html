Gaming

By far the hobby I've spent the most time on, I feel it would be unjust to not mention it. I've been playing games for as long as I can remember, so long in fact that it predates my ability to understand English by a year or two. My top three would be:


  1. Undertale
  2. Terraria
  3. Overwatch

I've spent literally thousands of hours on this hobby (no I don't have a problem! ...well, maybe) and still by far my favourite one would have to be Undertale. I could go on about it all day but in short it covers all bases: a great soundtrack, lovable cast, fun yet not easy gameplay and it has a meaningful message behind it. It's definitely a strange game at first glance but it has character and does what it sets out to do well. Even Pope Francis has a copy (it's true.)
